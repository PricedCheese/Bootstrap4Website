How You Can Make a difference

Yes it's true, yeah you reading this can make all the difference inclimate change by reading the examples below!

Recycle

The most effective ways to fight climate change are the 3 R's Reduce,Reuse and Recycle!

Bike

Instead of driving or using a vehicle that emmits C02 if you can try biking not only is it good for the enviroment but for your health as well.

No More Waste

Try not to waste any extra you have wether it be food or electronics.

Save Energy

Take a look at the labels on your appliances, and never leave them on standby. Always adjust the thermostat for heating and air conditioning.

Fly less

Air travel leaves behind a huge carbon footprint. Before you book your next airline ticket, consider greener options such as buses or trains, or try vacationing closer to home

By donating to the proper fondtations you can help them help the earth.

Research

By doing your own research you can find out many more ways that you can help in the fight against climate change.
